Frequently Asked Questions

Is this service really free?

Yes, LlamaDNS is completely free for personal use. We are open-source and supported by community donations.

How fast is propagation?

Updates are typically reflected globally within 60 seconds thanks to our low TTL settings and edge network.

Do you support IPv6?

Yes, we fully support AAAA records. You can update IPv4 and IPv6 addresses independently.
